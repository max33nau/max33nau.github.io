What is Bewander?

Bewander is a fresh social media website that was created to connect travelers to the best way to see the world on whatever budget. It strives to be a trusted source of reviews created by like-minded travelers and help travelers network while abroad. When you go somewhere new, you tend to ask your friends what they thought if they’ve been here. That’s what Bewander is. A place for you to get traveling advice you can rely on - without a vested interest.

Did I build Bewander from scratch?

No, when I was completing my course at Tech Academy (a bootcamp style coding course) we were given an existing project to work on. We basically treated the project as if we were working for a Client and a Product Owner that had a backlog of tasks we were supposed to complete. At Tech Academy, they want your final project to feel like a real job experience, so we were put in a scrum type atmosphere where we completed two sprints worth of work. We would assign ourselves tasks from the backlog and have a daily standup each day to discuss our progress.
